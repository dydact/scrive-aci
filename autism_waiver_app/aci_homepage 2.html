Our Programs

Autism Waiver (AW)

Comprehensive support services including Individual Intensive Support Services (IISS), Therapeutic Integration (TI), Respite Care, and Family Consultation to help individuals with autism thrive.

DDA Services

Developmental Disabilities Administration programs providing community support and residential habilitation for individuals with developmental disabilities.

Community First Choice

Personal care and companion services designed to support independent living and community integration for eligible individuals.

Community Services

Life skills training and behavioral support programs to enhance independence and quality of life for our clients.
